## Success Story – Teamwork & Presence of Mind 👏

I would like to share a great example of teamwork, awareness, and smart decision-making.

One of our existing customers, for whom we had previously provided AMC and Tally support, contacted us again for technical assistance. I connected with the customer and took remote access to check the issue.

During the troubleshooting process, I noticed that the Serial Number on the customer’s system was different from the Serial Number for which the AMC had originally been purchased. It was a completely different Serial Number.

With good presence of mind, I immediately informed to Yogesh Sir about the mismatch and clearly explained the situation to the customer. We then approached the customer regarding AMC coverage for the correct Serial Number.

Because of this timely observation, clear communication, and teamwork, we were successfully able to close a new AMC.

This is a great example of how attention to detail, awareness, and teamwork can help create new business opportunities.
